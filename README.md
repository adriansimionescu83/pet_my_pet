PetMyPet is a project created in collaboration with my colleagues Salam Baalbaki and Shuja Khan from Le Wagon bootcamp (https://www.lewagon.com) batch 792. In 4 days, our goal was to create an Airbnb-like application. We came up with a fun idea of renting a pet!

Is coming home to a pet a dream of yours? But your life is really busy or you travel a lot? Well, if you live for moments like walking a happy dog or a petting an adorable cat, you might get a chance to rent a pet of your dreams!

On the other hand, are you traveling for a while and want to give your pet a chance to meet new friends and not be sad while you're away?

With the PetMyPet web app, you can search for pets to rent that are in your own neighborhood! It helps connect pets, owners and borrowers who just want some company. Check out reviews for pet owners and renters before making your booking!

Happy pet renting!
